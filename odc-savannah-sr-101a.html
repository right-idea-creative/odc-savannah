Frequently Asked

Common questions about garage door repair

Is same-day service available?
Same-day service is available subject to technician availability. Call (912) 964-8459 and we will give you an accurate arrival window based on current schedule.
What does "$0 service call with repair" mean?
When you approve and complete a repair, the service call fee is waived. Your technician provides a written estimate before any work begins. If you choose not to proceed, a standard service call fee applies.
What does "non-commission technician" mean?
Most garage door companies pay technicians on commission — meaning they earn more when your repair costs more. Our technicians are salaried employees. Their incentive is accuracy, not upsell.
Is a broken spring dangerous?
Yes. Torsion springs are under significant tension. Do not attempt to open the door or repair the spring yourself. Leave the door in place and call us.
Do you service all brands?
Yes. We service all major brands — Clopay, Amarr, Wayne Dalton, LiftMaster, Genie, Chamberlain, and others. Most common parts are stocked on our trucks.
